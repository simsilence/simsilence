Sim Silence

Moving AI NPC (Unity)

This AI NPC Pack provides ready-to-use, modular AI systems for NPCs that can interact, converse, and react dynamically to the player and environment. Perfect for RPGs, open-world games, simulations, or survival games, this pack gives your game depth and realism with minimal setup.

Advanced AI Behaviors – Walking, running, texting and more.

Customizable Personalities – Easily configure traits like walking, running, jogging, etc.

Environment Awareness – NPCs can detect waypoints dynamically.

Animation Support – Includes Animator Controller examples for walk, run, and interaction states.

Inspector-Friendly – Intuitive editor tools for quick setup and tuning.
